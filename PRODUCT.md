# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Residents of Amaranta Residence ordering coffee from their units for door-to-door or lobby delivery.

## Product Purpose
Provide a seamless, premium ordering experience for the "Brew & Go" coffee service, enabling residents to browse the menu, order, and track delivery with minimal friction.

## Brand Personality
Modern, Minimal, Passionate.

## Anti-references
- Generic fast-food apps (e.g. McDonald's, Burger King)
- Cluttered food delivery platforms (e.g. Grab, Foodpanda)
- Loud, neon colors and cheap, cartoonish illustrations
- Sterile corporate websites
- Mass-market coffee chains (e.g. Starbucks, Dunkin')
- Heavy drop shadows and outdated gradients

## Design Principles
- **Refined Simplicity**: Avoid the "cluttered" feel of mass-market apps by focusing on a clean, minimal interface that respects the user's attention.
- **Passionate Craft**: Reflect the artisanal quality of the coffee through modern and minimal design choices, favoring authenticity over commercial "slop".
- **Resident-First Convenience**: Optimize for a high-end residential context where the ordering process should feel like an extension of the residence's premium services.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Accessible for residents of all ages.
- High contrast for readability.
- Clear, legible typography.
